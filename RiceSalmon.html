Salmon Rice Recipe

This is the tasty salmon rice that you could be eating if you follow this recipe step by step

How to make Salmon Rice

Inspired by the viral TikTok trend, this salmon rice bowl makes for a tasty lunch or dinner. With healthy ingredients like instant brown rice, salmon and veggies, you'll have a flavorful meal in just 25 minutes.

For this recipe you'll need:

  • 4 ounces of salmon
  • 1 teaspoon of avocado oil
  • 1 cup instant brown rice
  • 1 cup water
  • Mayonnaise
  • Siracha
  • Avocado
  • Cucumber

Steps

  1. Cook the salmon in a pre-heated at 400F oven
  2. Combine rice and water in a small saucepan
  3. Divide the rice between 2 bowls. Top with salmon, avocado, cucumber and kimchi
  4. Once the salmon is cooked, mix it with the rice
  5. Enjoy your dinner
